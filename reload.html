EVENT PHOTOGRAPHY

While I can carry the emotion of any event into my photographs, my favorite events to take on are music festivals, sport games, and weddings.

Is your band gracing the stage? - We'll make sure that your fans not only hear, but also see, and feel the music. "Live" will be their main preference when it comes to listening to your music.

Is your team up heading to finals? Well, from the facepaint to the sweat dripping off your player's brows, we'll capture every breath-taking moment so your fans, and generations to come never forget that game!

Tying the knot? - We'll make sure you see you wedding dress just as pristine as when you wore it, your husband just as handsome and sharp as when he turned to see his bride.

Got an occasion coming up? Want to immortalize the key moments, the ones that make you laugh, cry and feel grateful to have lived them? I'd be honored to take on this responsibility.

 

PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY

I have been the Director of Photography for several companies major product launches, such as Apple, LG, Nike, Volcom. My experience has taught me what it takes to bring out the best of a product to invoke a powerful emotional response.

Tech company? - We'll show the world just how frictionless your product is, and therefore how much more enjoyable theirs will b e.

Clothing company? - We'll put the spotlight on your threads to show people hottest trends, so they can feel confident, sexy, sharp, and on top of the world.

Whether your goal is to showcase your product's functions, or style, whatever your vision is, we will make sure your product will leave such a strong impression on your audience, that they have to head into your store to find out more about it!
